TOKIYO

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is a sprawling metropolis that blends modern innovation with rich tradition, making it one of the most vibrant and influential cities globally. Located on the eastern coast of the island of Honshu, Tokyo is home to over 14 million people, with its metropolitan area housing more than 37 million, making it one of the most populous urban areas in the world. As the political, economic, and cultural heart of Japan, Tokyo is a global financial hub, housing major international corporations, stock exchanges, and cutting-edge technology firms.

The city is renowned for its iconic landmarks such as the Tokyo Tower, Shibuya Crossing, and the serene Meiji Shrine, while its neighborhoods like Shinjuku, Shibuya, and Akihabara are known for their bustling energy, shopping, and entertainment. Tokyo is also famous for its world-class cuisine, particularly sushi, ramen, and tempura, as well as for being a center of fashion and pop culture, with anime, manga, and technology influencing global trends. Additionally, Tokyo boasts some of the world's top universities and institutions and is a leader in innovation, particularly in robotics and engineering.
