PARIS

Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most iconic and influential cities in the world, known for its rich history, culture, and art. Located on the banks of the River Seine in northern France, Paris is home to approximately 2.2 million people, with a metropolitan area exceeding 12 million. Often called the "City of Light," Paris has been a center of intellectual, political, and artistic activity for centuries. The city is famous for landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, the Louvre Museum, and the Champs-Élysées, as well as its beautiful parks, historic architecture, and romantic atmosphere.

Paris is a global center for art, fashion, and culture, hosting world-renowned events like Paris Fashion Week and the Cannes Film Festival. As a leading financial hub in Europe, it is home to major businesses, banks, and the European Union's headquarters. Paris is also a key player in global diplomacy, education, and tourism, attracting millions of visitors annually. The city boasts prestigious universities like the Sorbonne and is a major center for science and innovation. Paris’s distinctive charm, from its quaint cafés to its cutting-edge modernity, makes it one of the most visited and admired cities in the world.
